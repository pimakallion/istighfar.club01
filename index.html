What is Istighfar.club?

Istighfar.club is a youth-focused community of individuals committed to repentance, personal growth, and finding inner peace.

We support each other through daily reminders, mentorship, and hand-held guidance to overcome bad habits, toxic environments, and negative patterns.

Together, we're rewiring our minds and hearts, seeking Allah's forgiveness, and creating lives filled with barakah (blessings) and purpose.
